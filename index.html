What We Do

When all others fail, we don't.

Primary engineers, designs, and builds high-quality large and medium power transformers, distribution transformers, and specialty transformers for demanding customers with demanding deadlines.

We are the turn-key solution for all of your transformer needs: Spec assistance, fast quoting, transparent progress monitoring, manufacturing, testing, logistics, installation, warranty coverage, and service.

We've got you covered.

Why Use Primary?

Industry-Best Lead Times

Primary has manufacturing partnerships with some of the world's experienced and capable factories. Our factories have long histories in the US market and demonstrable track records exceeding IEEE requirements. We've vetted every factory on earth and formed partnerships with the top six facilities.

White Glove Design Service

Some of our customers are small teams and first-time buyers. We're happy to help customers develop and tweak their specifications with their project's requirements in mind. With specifications in hand, we custom design your unit(s) and quickly provide your fully delivered cost and lead time.

Track Your Order in Real Time

We provide our customers 24/7 visibility into the status of their order. Watch your transformer move through design, manufacturing, testing, and shipping.

Designed for Reliability

We design our transformers, optimize our supply chains, price our projects, and schedule our deliveries with predictability in mind.

24/7 Transformer Support

We are always available and eager to help, be that early in your procurement process or after the transformer is up and running.
